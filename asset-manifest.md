# Cindie Email Signature Generator Assets

## Brand Tokens

- Primary color: Midnight `#0B1020`
- Secondary color: Electric Blue `#2563FF`
- Supporting colors: Silver `#E5E7EB`, White `#FFFFFF`, Soft Gray `#F6F8FB`
- Gradient reference: Midnight `#0B1020` to `#1A237E`
- Typeface: SF Pro, with system fallbacks for email compatibility
- Signature phrase used: `Your Home Expert for Life.`
- Primary CTA label: `Browse Cindie`

## Extraction Notes

The supplied PDF artwork was flattened into PDF form drawing content instead of separate named image files. The source PNG crops were rendered from the PDF at 600 DPI. The production Cindie logo files were rebuilt as clean SVG plus high-resolution PNG for sharper use in the generator and email signature output.
